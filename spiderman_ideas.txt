Spider-Man Themed Feature Ideas for Portfolio:

1. Spiderweb Background (Neural Net Style):
   - Particles floating around the screen.
   - When they get close to each other, they connect with thin, web-like lines.
   - Fits perfectly with the AI/neural network theme while having a subtle Spider-Man vibe.

2. Spidey-Sense Easter Egg:
   - When hovering over certain "dangerous" or key elements (like a destructive action, or even just the contact button), the screen slightly tingles, vibrates, or flashes a very subtle red outline around the edges of the viewport.

3. Web-Shooter Cursor:
   - Clicking anywhere on the screen shoots a small web animation from the cursor that sticks to the background for a few seconds before fading away.

4. Occasional Spider-Man Animation:
   - A literal, tiny Spider-Man sprite or animation that swings across the screen randomly (e.g., once every few minutes) or when a specific sequence of keys is pressed (like typing 'thwip').
